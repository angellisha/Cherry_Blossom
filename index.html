Biography


Hi everyone. My name is Marelyne, and I am a clay doll artist. After training for a long time and creating I would like to share my masterpieces with a world.

Before I found out about clay I draw and enjoyed creating 3D figurines. I felt free when I created something, but it did not feel like really something special. I wanted more, maybe something new that can take all my time. And somewhere at that time I saw a really pretty doll. It was created with clay, paint, and a lot of fabric. When I looked how to do it, it looked really hard, and it could not be created in one day. That's exactly what I need!

In 2015, I discovered polymer clay and the world of doll art; there was no turning back. When I discovered doll sculpting, everything I had experienced came together to create this art form. Each one of my dolls is a one-of-a-kind made of polymer clay. Realism and beauty has been my goal. With the discovery of doll sculpting, my talent and passion merged.

And now I am embracing the world of clay with every mold and brushstroke. Living for the magic of turning imagination into reality, one doll at a time!
